A Bright Future For All Young People

Bright Future acts as a navigator who helps young people discover their life goals and provides them with the tool kit they need to get there. Our programs offer young people the life skills, mentoring, and exposure needed to build their own bright futures.

We Work with the entire Ecosystem to Build change that lasts

We have learned that building a good life and a livelihood starts young. Most children make decisions* at the age of 13 that eventually impact their career i.e. when they choose a subject stream. Our model walks with young people from the age of 13 up to the age of 25 if needed. We guide them on the many steps they need to take towards understanding their interests, feasible careers, and building their skills accordingly.

Your support helps us drive tangible change in each young person’s life by working with everyone involved - including their parents, teachers, communities, and panchayats, in addition to the young person.

*career decisions may be active or passive, informed or uninformed
